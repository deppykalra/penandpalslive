About Pen & Pals Live

Pen & Pals Live began as intimate poetry and music open mics in Warsaw. From restaurant showcases to Hindi comedy nights, we have grown into a cultural platform connecting artists and audiences.

We now collaborate on larger productions — bringing international talent to Poland.

About The Show

Come and enjoy the super relatable and super funny Gaurav Kapoor in his live show. One of the funniest and most prolific stand-up acts in India — with 1M+ subscribers on YouTube, 1M+ followers on Instagram, and an IMDB 8.2 rated special on Amazon Prime.

His massive live shows are making him a household name and proving that one can truly live his art if he is brutally honest. His journalism degree and retail job experience now seem like stories from another life — but Gaurav claims he still remembers all the keyboard shortcuts, just in case.
