About Pen & Pals Live

Pen & Pals Live began as intimate poetry and music gatherings in Warsaw. What started as small artistic salons has evolved into a cultural production platform bringing global voices to Poland.

We collaborate with artists, venues and production partners to deliver elevated live experiences — from stand-up comedy to literary showcases.
