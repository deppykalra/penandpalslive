About Pen & Pals Live

Pen & Pals Live began as intimate poetry and music open mics in Warsaw. From restaurant showcases to Hindi comedy nights, we have grown into a cultural platform connecting artists and audiences.

We now collaborate on larger productions — bringing international talent to Poland.
